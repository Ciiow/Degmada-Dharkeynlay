Degmada Dharkeynley

Degmada Dharkeynley waxay ka mid tahay degmooyinka magaalada Mogadishu waxay xigtaa dhanka koonfur galbeed,Degmada badkeeda waxuu garaa ilaa 67km2 oo balac ah & 15km2 oo joog ah, Waana degmo ku caan ah ciyaaraha kala duwan sida kubada cagta.

Degmada Dharkenley

Degmada dharkeynlay Waxay xigta dhanka konfur galbeed Waxay xaduud la leedahay 2 degmo oo kala ah:

  • wadajir
  • kaxda
